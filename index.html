About Me

Dr. A. Mohan is a passionate educator and researcher with expertise in Manufacturing Automation, Mechanics of Materials, Sustainable Manufacturing, Internet of Things, Manufacturing Technology, and related fields. With a strong commitment to student-centric teaching, Dr. Mohan employs innovative pedagogy methods, including case-based learning, problem-solving approaches, and technology integration, to ensure students grasp concepts effectively.

As a researcher, Dr. Mohan focuses on Low-cost Automation solutions for industries, Digital Twin, IoT for Mechanical Applications, Materials and Manufacturing, Robotics and more. He has contributed significantly to the academic community with publications in reputed journals and conferences. A dedicated mentor, Dr. Mohan emphasizes inclusivity in teaching, adapting to the needs of diverse learners and fostering an environment conducive to growth and innovation. He has a keen interest in curriculum design and has developed engaging course materials for subjects like Automation in Manufacturing, Mechatronics, and Mechanics of Materials, among others. With a vision to bridge academia and industry, Dr. Mohan continues to inspire students and contribute to advancing knowledge in his field.
